### Slide 8 — Business Model
Headline: "Multiple revenue streams. Zero cost to students."
Revenue streams:
1. Provider subscription: R499–R1,499/month per property listed
2. NSFAS transaction fee: 1.5% of NSFAS payments processed through platform
3. Holiday rental commission: 8% of short-term rental revenue (provider opt-in)
4. University SaaS: R15,000–R50,000/year per institution for compliance dashboard
5. Premium placement: Boosted listings for providers
Unit economics:
- Avg provider revenue: ~R1,000/month × 12 = R12,000/year per property
- NSFAS transaction: R4,500/month × 12 × 1.5% = R810/year per bed
- 1,000 beds on platform = R810,000/year in transaction fees alone
