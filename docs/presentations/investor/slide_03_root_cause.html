The Root Cause: A Fragmented, Analogue System

Three parties. Zero integration. Billions lost to inefficiency.

Students
  • Rely on informal WhatsApp groups and word-of-mouth to find housing
  • No centralized platform for verified, accredited listings
  • Vulnerable to scams and unsafe living conditions
Providers
  • Submit paper-based NSFAS claims through disconnected third-party portals
  • Suffer from 5% fee deductions by inefficient middlemen
  • Struggle with manual compliance and accreditation paperwork
Universities & NSFAS
  • No live data on student residence locations or compliance status
  • NSFAS currently investigating its own contracted tech middlemen (Jan 2026)
  • High administrative burden for DHET reporting
