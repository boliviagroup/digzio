Recommendation & Next Steps

Adopt the Two-Layer Stack as the Primary KYC Engine

We recommend replacing Onfido with the DHA + Smile Identity Two-Layer Stack for all student KYC verifications on Digzio.
  • ✓ Cost Efficiency: Saves $102,000 annually at scale, effectively funding a full engineering hire.
  • ✓ Superior Security: Eliminates OCR forgery risks by validating directly against the government population register.
  • ✓ POPIA Compliance: Achieves 100% compliance regarding image storage by retaining zero ID document photos.
