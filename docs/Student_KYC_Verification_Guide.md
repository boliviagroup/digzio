# Digzio Student KYC Verification Guide

Welcome to the Digzio Student KYC Verification Guide. This document provides step-by-step instructions on how to complete your Know Your Customer (KYC) verification on the Digzio platform. Completing your KYC verification is a critical step in securing your student accommodation, as providers require it before approving applications.

## Why Complete KYC Verification?

The KYC verification process ensures a safe and trusted environment for both students and property providers. By verifying your identity, you demonstrate credibility, which significantly improves your chances of having your accommodation applications approved. Unverified profiles may experience delays or rejections, as providers prioritize applicants with confirmed identities.

## Step-by-Step Verification Process

### Step 1: Access Your Student Dashboard

To begin the verification process, you must first log in to your Digzio account. Navigate to the Digzio homepage at [www.digzio.co.za](https://www.digzio.co.za) and click the **Sign In** button. Enter your registered email address and password. Once logged in, click on **My Dashboard** in the top navigation bar to access your student portal.

### Step 2: Locate the KYC Verification Banner

On your Student Dashboard, look for the KYC verification banner located just below the application statistics section. The banner will display your current verification status. If you have not yet started the process, you will see an orange banner with the text **"Complete your KYC verification"** and a **"Verify Now"** button.

### Step 3: Open the Verification Form

Click the **"Verify Now"** button within the orange banner. This action will open the secure KYC Verification modal on your screen. The modal is designed to collect essential identity information while keeping your data encrypted and protected.

### Step 4: Complete the Required Fields

Fill out the verification form accurately. The following fields are required:

| Field Name | Description | Example |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| **ID Type** | Select your form of identification from the dropdown menu (South African ID, Passport, or Asylum Seeker Permit). | South African ID |
| **ID / Passport Number** | Enter your official identification number exactly as it appears on your document. | 9001015009087 |
| **Date of Birth** | Provide your date of birth using the calendar picker. | 01/01/1990 |
| **Institution Name** | Enter the name of the university or college you are attending (Optional but recommended). | University of Johannesburg |
| **Student Number** | Enter your official student number (Optional but recommended). | 202312345 |

Please ensure all information matches your official documents to avoid delays in the review process.

### Step 5: Submit Your Information

After reviewing your entries for accuracy, click the **"Submit KYC"** button at the bottom of the form. The system will process your submission securely. Upon successful submission, you will see a confirmation message indicating that your documents are under review.

## Tracking Your Verification Status

Once submitted, your KYC status will update on your Student Dashboard. You can track the progress by observing the banner:

- **Under Review:** An orange banner indicating that your submission has been received and is currently being processed by the Digzio team.
- **KYC Verified:** A green banner confirming that your identity has been successfully verified. Providers can now see your verified status when reviewing your applications.

If you encounter any issues during the verification process or if your status remains "Under Review" for an extended period, please contact Digzio support for assistance.
